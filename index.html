Prayer rule of thumb: stay 5+ nights in one place → resident, pray in full. On short stops with travel (4 nights or fewer) → traveler, you may shorten (Qasr) and combine (Jam’). Daily prayer times are in the separate file.

On the road or in the air you're a traveler: shorten 4-rak'ah prayers to 2 (Qasr) and combine Dhuhr+Asr & Maghrib+Isha (Jam'). Fajr stays 2 rak'ah and is never combined. In a plane, pray at each prayer's time for the land below (ask crew for the qibla); sit if you can't stand.
